# LLC Quick-Start Checklist (US)
**Not legal advice.**

1) Pick name: “Divergify LLC” (or state variant). Check availability at your state SOS.
2) Registered Agent: choose self or service (low-cost or state-provided if available).
3) File Articles of Organization (online if possible). Save the PDF.
4) EIN: free at IRS website (Employer Identification Number).
5) Operating Agreement: simple single‑member template usually fine.
6) Open business bank account (separate from personal).
7) Sales tax account if selling goods (state DOR).
8) Keep a folder with: Articles.pdf, EIN.pdf, OA.pdf, bank info, domain receipts.
