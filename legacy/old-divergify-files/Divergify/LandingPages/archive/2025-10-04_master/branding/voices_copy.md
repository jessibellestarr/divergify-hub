# Divergify Voices & Copy Bank
_Last updated: 2025-10-04_

## Voice A — Blunt Friendly
**Vibe:** Fast, witty, a little salty. Speaks like the smart friend who already has a whiteboard marker in hand.
**Use when:** Social posts, product updates, onboarding flows.

**Snippets**
- **Mini bio:** "Divergify is the momentum dojo for brains that zig when the world zags."
- **CTA:** "Let’s beat yesterday by three percent. Tap in."
- **Email opener:** "Quick hit: here’s the new trick to keep gremlins from hijacking your focus bubble."
- **Social proof line:** "10,492 chaos pilots tried it. 10,491 came back for seconds. (One got distracted.)"

## Voice B — Warm Coach
**Vibe:** Encouraging, validating, grounded. Reminds you to hydrate and ship the thing.
**Use when:** Welcome sequence, onboarding scripts, community guidelines.

**Snippets**
- **Mini bio:** "We build tools and language so neurodivergent brains can move with grace, not guilt."
- **CTA:** "Ready to make your next tiny move? Start here."
- **Email opener:** "Hey crew — take a deep breath. You’re exactly where you need to be to take the next step."
- **Check-in text:** "What’s one win you want to celebrate from today? Hit reply and brag."

## Voice C — Investor-Ready
**Vibe:** Confident, metrics-aware, still human. Reads like a founder who can handle diligence decks.
**Use when:** Pitch materials, partnership decks, about page for stakeholders.

**Snippets**
- **Mini bio:** "Divergify delivers momentum tools for the 33% of knowledge workers with neurodivergent traits."
- **CTA:** "Review the traction snapshot."
- **Email opener:** "Attaching our latest growth metrics and roadmap priorities for Q3."
- **Impact line:** "Users increase shipped tasks by 27% average within the first month while reporting lower burnout risk."

## Reusable Bits & Switches
- **Taglines:** "Rerouting genius on detour." / "Structure that forgives, systems that work."
- **Tin Foil Hat reminder:** "Privacy-first, opt-out ready. Nothing leaves your hands unless you say so."
- **CTA variations:**
  1. "Summon the focus bubble"
  2. "Download the baseline kit"
  3. "Book a Divergify walkthrough"
- **Follow-up nudge:** "Still interested? I saved your progress — one click and you’re back in."
- **Launch teaser:** "Next drop: Momentum Ledger templates + automation recipes."

## Formatting Notes
- Keep sentences short (under 18 words) for Voice A. Add breathing room for Voice B.
- Lead with outcome, follow with humor (if any).
- Avoid clichés like "crushing it" unless quoting a user.
