Store (Print-on-Demand)

Drop your merch pipeline here. Privacy-first, zero inventory stress.

Plug-and-play options

  1. Ko‑fi Shop: Create or update your products → copy the Shop link → paste it below as a `` tag. Instant fallback.
  2. Printful / Printify: Use their iframe embed. Replace the placeholder `
    ` with the embed snippet.
  3. Fallback link: If embeds fail, keep the button live with a friendly redirect to your storefront.

Embed placeholder. Example:

<iframe src="https://ko-fi.com/divergify/shop" width="100%" height="600" frameborder="0"></iframe>
