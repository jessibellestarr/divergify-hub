# LLC Quick-Start Checklist (US)
_Not legal advice. Always confirm with your state requirements._

1. **Name search:** Confirm “Divergify LLC” (or your variant) is available with your Secretary of State.
2. **Registered agent:** Choose yourself, a trusted human, or a low-cost registered agent service.
3. **Articles of Organization:** File online if possible. Save the approved PDF + receipt.
4. **EIN:** Apply free at IRS.gov (takes ~5 minutes). Print and store the confirmation letter.
5. **Operating agreement:** Draft a simple single-member (or multi-member) agreement and sign it.
6. **State tax accounts:** Register for sales tax, employer withholding, or franchise taxes if required.
7. **Business bank account:** Bring Articles, EIN, and ID to open a dedicated account. Keep personal and business funds separate.
8. **Licenses & permits:** Check city/county requirements (general business, home occupation, resale certificates).
9. **Bookkeeping setup:** Pick a simple ledger (Wave, QuickBooks, or spreadsheet). Track expenses from day one.
10. **Compliance calendar:** Note annual report deadlines, state fees, and tax filings. Set reminders.
11. **Document vault:** Store Articles.pdf, EIN.pdf, OperatingAgreement.pdf, bank info, domain receipts, and major vendor contracts in one shared folder.
