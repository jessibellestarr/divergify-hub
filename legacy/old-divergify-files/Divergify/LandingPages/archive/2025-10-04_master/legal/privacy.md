# Privacy Notice (Plain-Language Draft)
_Not legal advice. Review with counsel before launch._

## What we collect
- Info you submit on forms (usually an email address and optional message).
- Basic site analytics from privacy-respecting tools only. No invasive trackers.

## How we use it
- Send product updates or respond to what you asked for.
- Improve Divergify content and tooling in aggregate.
- Never sold, rented, or traded. Period.

## Storage & retention
- Launch list lives in Netlify Forms or our encrypted inbox.
- Tin Foil Hat Mode lets you skip the form and email us directly. We honor deletion requests fast.
- We purge dormant contacts at least twice a year.

## Third parties
- Hosting: Netlify or GitHub Pages.
- Email: Fastmail or another privacy-first provider once selected.
- Payments/Merch (optional): Ko‑fi, Printful, or Printify — managed on their platforms with their policies.

## Your choices
- Unsubscribe links in every email.
- Email `privacy@divergify.app` to request a copy or deletion. Expect a reply within 5 business days.
- You can use Tin Foil Hat Mode (offline instructions) anytime.

## Changes
We’ll update this draft as the stack evolves and keep a changelog in the repo.
